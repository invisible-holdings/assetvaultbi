Welcome to Asset Vault BI

Asset Vault BI is a global platform dedicated to helping individuals and organizations identify, verify, and recover unclaimed funds from around the world. These funds may include dormant bank accounts, uncashed checks, insurance payouts, pensions, dividends, tax refunds, and other financial assets that have gone uncollected over time.

Our team specializes in conducting comprehensive searches across multiple jurisdictions, leveraging global financial databases, public registries, and institutional records to trace assets that rightfully belong to you. We work closely with financial institutions, regulatory bodies, and government-held asset registries to ensure accurate identification and recovery.

Asset recovery agents like Asset Vault BI play a critical role in:

  • Investigating dormant and lost financial records
  • Matching individuals or businesses to rightful assets
  • Assisting with legal documentation and verification
  • Facilitating claims across different countries and legal systems
  • Providing continuous monitoring for newly identified assets

With a strong focus on confidentiality, compliance, and transparency, Asset Vault BI simplifies what is often a complex and time-consuming process, giving clients confidence and peace of mind.
