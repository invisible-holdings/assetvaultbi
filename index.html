Asset Vault BI

Asset Vault BI is a global platform dedicated to helping individuals and organizations identify, verify, and recover unclaimed funds worldwide.

  • Investigating dormant and lost financial records
  • Matching individuals or businesses to rightful assets
  • Assisting with legal documentation and verification
  • Facilitating international claims
  • Continuous monitoring for new assets

Asset Recovery Agent Training

This training program equips individuals with the knowledge and practical skills required to identify, trace, and recover unclaimed financial assets globally.

What You Will Learn

  • Understanding unclaimed assets and financial systems
  • Advanced global asset search techniques
  • Use of financial and public databases
  • KYC and client verification procedures
  • Legal frameworks across jurisdictions
  • Claims preparation and submission
  • Professional ethics and confidentiality

Opportunities

Participants gain the ability to work independently or collaborate with institutions in asset tracing and recovery projects worldwide.
